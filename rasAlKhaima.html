Best Tours and Activities in Ras Al Khaimah
Think beyond Dubai, think Ras Al Khaimah tours. Surrounded by the gorgeous mountain scenery all around, the beauty of this northern most emirate is worth every bit your time. Ras Al Khaimah sightseeing ranges from pristine sandy beaches, fierce mountains, hot springs of the sun-baked desert, and sprawling oases that can make you weak on your knees. A perfect destination for a luxury vacation, activities around Ras Al Khaimah is sure to make your holiday memorable.
The adventure enthusiasts love to come to this sprawling oases and enjoy some of the best adventure beach sports. The small emirate has it all, from desert safaris to relaxed hot spring baths, adventure sports to serene beaches. Ras Al Khaimah is one of the most frequented Dubai weekend getaways for the tourists and locals alike.
Giving you the best of Ras Al Khaimah sightseeing, Rayna tours brings to different façades of this exceptional luxury destination through affordable tours. Enjoy day trips to the best of city sights and exclusive entertainment adventures such as jet skiing, parasailing, dhow cruise and more. From the must-see attractions of The National Museum, Khatt Hot Springs, Shimal, and Dhayah fort to the best things to do like the Falcon Show, Al Sawan Race track, Saqr park, Iceland waterpark and more; Ras Al Khaimah surprises you with its incredibility at every step.
Ras Al Khaimah boasts the best of natural and manmade excursions to make your trip memorable. It is one of the most visited weekend getaways by the locals and beckons you to explore, experience and enjoy its beauty today!
