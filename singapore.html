Best Tours and Activities in Singapore
Singapore, in the vibrant heart of Southeast Asia, is one of the world’s most visited tourist destinations. It may be a small nation, but this city-state effortlessly bundles a wealth of attractions and experiences into its compact size. Whether you are seeking a quality family vacation, an adventurous getaway, or a laid-back, romantic break, we serve as the ultimate tour agency Singapore to cater to every interest.
Embark on a fully guided Singapore city tour with roundtrip transfers to explore a wealth of treasures. Stop at the legendary Merlion Park, appreciate the towering structures that dot Marina Bay, and immerse yourself in a kaleidoscope of cultures at bustling Little India, Chinatown, Arab Quarter, etc. Reserve for yourself the most astonishing standpoint by securing tickets to Marina Bay Sands Sky Park or enjoying a relaxed spin on the iconic Singapore Flyer.
Singapore is acknowledged as a city in a garden, which is reflected in its natural splendors lying right in its heart. Experience this Singapore tour first-hand as you step into the lush oasis of Gardens by the Bay or lose yourself in the captivating charm of over 150-year-old Singapore Botanic Gardens. It is also home to some of the most fascinating wildlife attractions, mainly Bird Paradise Singapore, Singapore Zoo, Singapore River Wonders, and Singapore River Wonders.
With a vast array of attractions awaiting at every turn and twist, a big bus tour Singapore is one of the most flexible ways to experience them all at your own pace. Enjoy the chance to appreciate the region’s regal past, stunning landmarks, and fascinating multicultural environment while traveling aboard a double-decker bus with an open top. It offers four ticket options, covers over 30 stops (in two diverse routes), and comes with multilingual commentary onboard (available in 12 languages).
An inescapable part of any Singapore holiday is visiting Sentosa Island. This island paradise is 500 hectares of pure bliss and excitement with its myriad offerings, from the breathtaking SEA Aquarium to daredevil’s favorites like iFly Singapore and AJ Hackett Sentosa. Watch the classic Wings of Time, dive into movie-themed thrills at Universal Studios, or enter Adventure Cover Water Park for a water adventure like no other. Join a Singapore tour guide, set out on a cable car ride, or take to SkyHelix Sentosa for an unparalleled exploration of this absolute fun and entertainment destination.
Our Singapore portfolio also includes Singapore River Cruise packages, promising a relaxed and enlightening sightseeing experience from the region’s water onboard an eco-friendly bumboat. That’s not all; we offer various transfers to make your Singapore travel seamless, such as airport pick-up and drop-off. There are dedicated airport transfers available for late-night or early-morning arrivals and departures. You can also count on our intracity transfers to get around in the city and exclusive port transfers to travel between the airport and the cruise terminal.
Whatever the purpose of your Singapore travel, our extensive repertoire of activities and excursions appeals to all types of visitors. All you need to do is inquire with us online or speak to one of our representatives to choose or customize your perfect Singapore tour.
